About Me

Get to know the developer behind the code

My name is Molly Corbett. I was born in Potsdam, New York, on June 18, 2009. My father is Sean Corbett and my mother was Jessica Corbett. My parents divorced when I was about three or four years old, and I lived a few days with each of my parents each week until I was 8. Shortly after turning 8, my mother moved all the way to Texas, taking my much older half-sister and my much younger half-sister with her. I then lived only with my dad and my brother Patrick, who is a year-and-a-half older than me.

I had no technological experience other than the basic uses of a computer or phone before I started this course. I now know how to code in HTML/CSS, and I am learning how to program in Python, as well as learning to write command lines. I wanted to take this Computer and Information Science course because I knew I wanted to go to SUNY Canton for something computer-related, and this class would prepare me for college.

When my parents lived together, we had two dogs - a big mop dog and a beagle named Teddy. The big mop dog died, but Teddy lived to be 14 years old. When my parents moved apart, my mom got more dogs, as well as cats, chickens, and some rabbits. I got to have my own cat, an orange cat named Percy, as well as an albino rabbit named Snowball. Percy loved me, but Snowball did not.

At three years old, I went to a preparatory daycare to learn manners and structure before enrolling in Pre-K at St. Lawrence Elementary at four years old. I was in the morning pre-k. I moved on to A.A.K. Middle School, where I joined band as a flute player until the Covid pandemic hit on Friday the 13th of my fifth grade year, and we all went to online learning for the next year.

When I was in elementary school, I had a friend named Alex, who played with me all day, every day. For a few years, I had no friends because Alex moved away to Parishville, then came back a couple years later. Recently, Alex moved back to Potsdam again, but we both have other friends now. In my first year of high school at Potsdam High School, I met Lila, and we have been best friends since. We go to the same clubs, have the same hobbies, as well as the same interests.

I go to FFA, Spanish Club, and GSA with Lila, and I go to NAHS and Artistic Congress by myself. In BOCES, I go to SkillsUSA as the treasurer and Student Leader meetings. Through BOCES, I am taking an Introduction to Python course online at SUNY Canton, and next year I will also be taking Introduction to Cybersecurity and one other course through SUNY Canton and BOCES as well. Once I graduate with honors next year, I will go to SUNY Canton for a double major in Cybersecurity and Data Science & Analytics. I will get a Bachelor's Degree in Data Science & Analytics, as well as a Master’s in Cybersecurity, since SUNY Canton offers an accelerated Masters Degree for Cybersecurity. In ten years, I will be working for the government, for either the CIA or the Department of Defense.
